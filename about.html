About me

I am Erica M. Ponlaon—a freelance web developer, virtual assistant, and creative collaborator based in the Philippines, working with clients worldwide.

Background

My work combines technical execution with clear communication. I started by learning how websites are structured, then expanded into supporting real operations: inboxes, calendars, light research, and visual assets that match a brand’s tone.

Today I focus on helping small businesses, educators, and independent professionals who need dependable delivery without hiring a full in-house team. Whether the priority is a new landing page, a cleaner workflow, or consistent social graphics, I aim to make the next step obvious.

How I work with clients

  • Start with a short discovery pass: goals, audience, timeline, and existing assets.
  • Agree on milestones and a single channel for day-to-day questions (usually email).
  • Share work in small increments so you can review early—before design or copy is locked in.
  • Close each project with a concise handoff: what was delivered, where files live, and suggested next steps.

Tools & environment

Browser-based design review, Git-based code when applicable, cloud folders for assets, and task boards when projects grow past a few deliverables. I am comfortable adapting to your stack where it is reasonable.
